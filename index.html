Who We Are

A brotherhood of experienced men building lasting friendships

A Brotherhood of Experienced Men

The Senior Men's Club of Wilmington is for men 55 years and older and is about fellowship. We've been bringing men together since 1988 — that's 37 years of friendship, learning, and community.

Every Friday, men from all walks of life come together to enjoy coffee, conversation, and compelling speakers. No pressure, no politics, no sales pitches — just genuine connection with like-minded individuals.

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about joining the SMC

Are you affiliated with any church?

No. Lifepoint Church generously hosts our meetings, but we are completely independent and nondenominational. All faiths and backgrounds are welcome.

What are the dues?

$50 per year — less than $1 per week. This covers everything: coffee, donuts, programs, and all club activities. It's made possible by our dedicated volunteer members.

Can I bring a friend or visitor?

Absolutely! Guests are always welcome. It's a great way to experience what we're all about before joining. Just show up any Friday morning.

What's the age requirement?

The Senior Men's Club is for men 55 years and older. We welcome men from all backgrounds and walks of life.

What if I can't make every meeting?

No problem! Come when you can. There's no attendance requirement. Many members travel or have other commitments — we understand life happens.

What topics are discussed?

Our speakers cover everything from local history and health to finance, travel, military affairs, and community issues. The only topics we avoid are politics and religion — it's what keeps us united.

Ready to Join Us?

Come visit a meeting and see what the Senior Men's Club is all about. No commitment required — just show up any Friday morning and introduce yourself.
